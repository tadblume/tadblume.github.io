What is it?

Rate Your Music is a free to use website that provides users with the ability to rate music, review music, create lists about favorite albums or bands, and much more. The website was created on December 24, 2000 and currently has an active user base of over 650,000 people.


Charts

The Rym (rateyourmusic) charts are a section of the website that is dedicated to showing you the best albums of all time as well as the best albums of any year or decade that you choose. You can use this feature to good music that you may not have heard about.
